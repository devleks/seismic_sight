# SeismicSight - 4 Minute Demo Video Script

**Target Duration:** ~4 Minutes
**Tone:** Professional, informative, and urgent but reassuring.
**Visuals:** Screen recordings of the SeismicSight application running on both desktop and mobile devices.

---

## 0:00 - 0:30 | Introduction & Overview
**Visual:** Title card: "SeismicSight: Emergency Disaster Guardian." Cut to a wide shot of the SeismicSight dashboard showing the live camera feed and USGS data panel.
**Audio (Narrator):**
"Welcome to SeismicSight, your elite Emergency Disaster Guardian. In a world where seismic events can strike without warning, preparedness is your best defense. SeismicSight leverages advanced AI vision and live seismic data to provide real-time structural hazard analysis and predictive disaster simulations. Today, we'll walk you through how this powerful tool can help you identify risks in your environment and prepare for the unexpected."

## 0:30 - 1:00 | Setup & Secure API Key Management
**Visual:** Show the initial load screen. Zoom in on the "Set API Key" modal. Show a user entering a key, which is masked (dots instead of text).
**Audio (Narrator):**
"Getting started is quick and secure. SeismicSight requires a Google Gemini API key to power its AI features. You can enter your key via our secure, masked modal dialog, protecting it from shoulder-surfing. For developers, the key can also be configured via environment variables for seamless deployment. Once authenticated, you're ready to start scanning."

## 1:00 - 1:45 | Real-Time Hazard Detection & Camera Switching
**Visual:** Switch to a mobile device view. The app automatically opens the back camera. The user pans around a room. The user adjusts the "Confidence Threshold" slider. The user taps the "Scan" button. The "Live Hazard Feed" populates with identified risks (e.g., unanchored bookshelf, glass window).
**Audio (Narrator):**
"Let's look at Real-Time Hazard Detection. On mobile and tablet devices, SeismicSight intelligently defaults to the back camera for optimal scanning. You can also seamlessly switch between the front and back cameras at any time. 
By pointing your camera at your surroundings and initiating a scan, the AI analyzes the room for structural and environmental risks—like unanchored furniture or exposed glass. You can even adjust the AI's confidence threshold to fine-tune sensitivity and focus on the most critical hazards. The Live Hazard Feed logs these risks chronologically, providing a Structural Integrity Index and actionable mitigation strategies."

## 1:45 - 2:30 | Voice Commands & USGS Data Integration
**Visual:** The user clicks the microphone icon. Text appears on screen showing the voice command: "Where were there earthquakes above magnitude 5 today?" The left panel updates with live USGS data.
**Audio (Narrator):**
"In an emergency, hands-free operation is crucial. SeismicSight features robust Voice Commands. You can simply ask the AI to 'Scan the room,' 'Simulate an earthquake,' or 'Stop live view.' 
Additionally, SeismicSight integrates directly with the US Geological Survey API. You can ask complex questions like, 'Were there any earthquakes above magnitude 5 today?' The AI will fetch live seismic data, display the most recent significant events, and provide immediate safety protocols based on global activity."

## 2:30 - 3:30 | Predictive Disaster Simulation
**Visual:** The user says "Simulate an earthquake." The screen transitions to a side-by-side comparison. The left side shows the original room; the right side shows a highly realistic, AI-generated aftermath of the room post-earthquake (e.g., fallen books, cracked walls).
**Audio (Narrator):**
"One of SeismicSight's most powerful features is the Predictive Disaster Simulation. By analyzing your current room layout, the AI generates a realistic, side-by-side visual simulation of the potential aftermath of a major seismic event. 
This visceral comparison helps users truly understand the impact of unmitigated hazards, reinforcing the importance of securing heavy furniture and clearing exit paths before a disaster strikes."

## 3:30 - 4:00 | Disclaimer, Hackathon Call-Out & Outro
**Visual:** The screen fades to a text overlay highlighting the "Disclaimer of Liability" and the "Apache License 2.0" badge. It then transitions to a graphic for the "Gemini Live Agent Challenge" on Devpost.
**Audio (Narrator):**
"It is important to note that SeismicSight's predictive simulations are for educational and training purposes only—they are not real-time events. In an actual seismic emergency, always follow local authority guidelines and established evacuation protocols immediately. 
SeismicSight is proudly open-source and licensed under the Apache 2.0 License. We built this project as a submission for the Gemini Live Agent Challenge on Devpost—check out our project at geminiliveagentchallenge.devpost.com!
Prepare today, protect tomorrow. Thank you for watching."
